# Beef Wellington Recipe

## Ingredients
- 1.5 to 2 lbs (700g to 900g) beef tenderloin (center-cut)
- Olive oil
- Salt and black pepper
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 6 oz (170g) pâté (preferably mushroom or foie gras)
- 8 oz (225g) cremini or button mushrooms, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 shallot, finely chopped
- 6-8 slices prosciutto or Parma ham
- Puff pastry sheet (enough to wrap the beef)
- 1 egg (beaten, for egg wash)

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Season beef tenderloin with salt and pepper.
3. Heat olive oil in a pan over high heat. Sear beef on all sides until browned (about 2-3 minutes per side). Remove and let cool. Brush with Dijon mustard.
4. In the same pan, add garlic, shallot, and mushrooms. Cook until all moisture evaporates and mushroom mixture is dry. Let cool.
5. Lay out prosciutto slices on cling film, overlapping to form a rectangle. Spread mushroom mixture on top, then place beef on it and wrap tightly using cling film. Chill for 15 minutes.
6. Roll out puff pastry on a floured surface. Unwrap beef from cling film and place in center of pastry.
7. Wrap beef tightly in pastry, trimming excess and sealing edges with egg wash.
8. Brush pastry with egg wash, make a few slits on top for steam to escape.
9. Bake in preheated oven for 25-30 minutes until pastry is golden and beef is cooked to desired doneness.
10. Rest for 10 minutes before slicing.

## Tips
- Use a meat thermometer for perfect doneness: 125°F (52°C) for rare, 135°F (57°C) for medium rare.
- Let beef rest before slicing to retain juices.

Enjoy your classic and elegant Beef Wellington!
