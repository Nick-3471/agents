# Michelin Star Restaurants in Colorado

## Overview
As of the 2025 Michelin Guide, Colorado has 9 restaurants with Michelin Star ratings. The Michelin Guide has announced it will expand coverage to all of Colorado in the 2026 edition, potentially recognizing additional restaurants across the state.

---

## Two Michelin Stars ⭐⭐

### The Wolf's Tailor
- **Stars:** ⭐⭐ (2 Michelin Stars)
- **Location:** Denver, CO (4058 Tejon St., Denver, CO 80211)
- **Cuisine Type:** Contemporary, Creative
- **Notes:** Colorado's first and only two-star Michelin restaurant. Offers innovative, multi-course tasting experiences that revolve around two prominent seasons, spotlighting garden-fresh produce and seasonal ingredients. Chef: Taylor Stark

---

## One Michelin Star ⭐

### Alma Fonda Fina
- **Stars:** ⭐ (1 Michelin Star)
- **Location:** Denver, CO (2556 15th St., Denver 80211)
- **Cuisine Type:** Mexican, Contemporary
- **Notes:** Contemporary Mexican restaurant by Chef Johnny Curiel. Known for creative, high-quality Mexican fare that pays homage to his culturally rich heritage. More affordable than other starred establishments.

### Beckon
- **Stars:** ⭐ (1 Michelin Star)
- **Location:** Denver, CO (2843 Larimer St., Denver 80205) - RiNo District
- **Cuisine Type:** Contemporary, New American, Scandinavian
- **Notes:** Hyper-local tasting menu restaurant. Chef Peter Briggs leads the kitchen with a menu that changes quarterly with the seasons. Known for exceptional service.

### Bosq
- **Stars:** ⭐ (1 Michelin Star)
- **Location:** Aspen, CO (312 S. Galena St., Aspen, CO 81611)
- **Cuisine Type:** Contemporary, Tasting Menu
- **Notes:** Aspen's first and only Michelin-starred restaurant. Led by Chef and Owner Barclay Dodge. Offers thoughtfully composed tasting menu experiences with a focus on high-quality ingredients.

### Brutø
- **Stars:** ⭐ (1 Michelin Star)
- **Location:** Denver, CO (1801 Blake St., Denver 80202)
- **Cuisine Type:** Contemporary, Brutalist American, Scandinavian
- **Notes:** Known for its moody Scandinavian aesthetic and Costa Rican-inspired tasting menus with creative drink pairings. Also holds a Michelin Green Star for sustainability. Specializes in artisanal bread and cheese.

### Frasca Food and Wine
- **Stars:** ⭐ (1 Michelin Star)
- **Location:** Boulder, CO (1738 Pearl St., Boulder 80302)
- **Cuisine Type:** Italian (Friuli-Venezia Giulia)
- **Notes:** James Beard Award-winning restaurant specializing in refined food and wine from northeast Italy. Offers exceptional Italian dishes paired with wines from the Friuli-Venezia Giulia region.

### Kizaki
- **Stars:** ⭐ (1 Michelin Star)
- **Location:** Denver, CO (1551 S. Gaylord St., Denver, CO)
- **Cuisine Type:** Japanese, Sushi, Omakase
- **Notes:** Colorado's only Michelin-starred sushi restaurant. Chef Toshi Kizaki earned his star within months of opening. Known for decades of discipline, artistry, and mastery in Japanese cuisine. Features fresh fish flown in from Japan.

### Margot
- **Stars:** ⭐ (1 Michelin Star)
- **Location:** Denver, CO (1551 S Pearl St., Denver 80210)
- **Cuisine Type:** Contemporary American
- **Notes:** Elevated contemporary American dining with multiple tasting menu options. Offers both five-course and 12-course chef's tasting menus plus à la carte dining from a tiny kitchen. Chef: Justin Fulton (local chef with true Denver roots).

### Mezcaleria Alma
- **Stars:** ⭐ (1 Michelin Star)
- **Location:** Denver, CO (2550 15th St., Denver)
- **Cuisine Type:** Mexican, Contemporary
- **Notes:** Part of Chef Johnny Curiel's award-winning restaurant group. Captures the vibrant spirit of Mexico City with CDMX-inspired small plates and an extensive Mexican spirits program. Features a seafood-forward menu with in-house aging and brining program.

## 2026 Expansion

The Michelin Guide announced it will expand its coverage to the **entire state of Colorado** in the 2026 edition. This expansion opens the door for restaurants in previously uncovered areas such as:
- Colorado Springs
- Fort Collins
- Vail
- Beaver Creek
- Snowmass Village
- And other regions throughout the state

This expansion is expected to bring recognition to additional high-quality restaurants across Colorado.
