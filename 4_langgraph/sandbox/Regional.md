## Overview
The Regional is a family-owned, from-scratch restaurant located in the charming Old Town Fort Collins. The restaurant specializes in hometown American comfort food featuring locally-sourced ingredients from the farm, ranch, and dock. With a philosophy that "The Regional tastes like home," the establishment has become a beloved destination for both locals and visitors seeking authentic, quality dining.

---

## Menu

### Concept
The Regional features a rotating, seasonal menu that changes constantly, ensuring fresh ingredients and creative dishes. All food is locally sourced and carefully curated. The restaurant offers:

- **Scratch-made American comfort food**
- **House-made pastas**
- **Fresh oysters**
- **Gluten-free options**
- **Seasonal dishes**
- **Handcrafted cocktails**

### Featured Menu Items
- **Reggie Burger** - A signature item featuring quality beef
- **Spicy Fried Chicken Sandwich** - Known for its bold flavors
- **Sunchoke Elote** - A creative vegetable preparation
- **Regional Salad** - House specialty salad
- **Ham of the Day** - Daily rotating ham selection
- **Fresh Oysters** - Premium oyster offerings
- **Grits** - Southern-style comfort side
- **Mussels Dish** - Recent menu addition
- **Salmon** - Farm-to-table preparation
- **Handcrafted Cocktails** - Including signature Negroni (Campari, Juniper Jones gin)

### Dietary Accommodations
- **Gluten-Free Options:** The restaurant features dedicated gluten-free fryers and has an extensive gluten-free menu suitable for those with celiac disease
- Staff is knowledgeable and accommodating regarding dietary restrictions
