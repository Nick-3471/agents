# Top 10 Zoos in the United States

## 1. Omaha's Henry Doorly Zoo and Aquarium
**Location:** Omaha, Nebraska

Omaha's Henry Doorly Zoo and Aquarium is consistently ranked as the #1 zoo in the United States. Known for its innovative exhibits and world-class animal care, the zoo features the famous Desert Dome and a diverse collection of animals from around the globe. The facility combines both zoo and aquarium experiences, making it a comprehensive wildlife destination.

---

## 2. Cheyenne Mountain Zoo
**Location:** Colorado Springs, Colorado

Ranked #2 in USA TODAY's 10Best Readers' Choice Awards 2026, Cheyenne Mountain Zoo is situated at an elevation of 6,800 feet and offers stunning mountain views. The zoo is known for its Rocky Mountain Wild exhibit and exceptional animal care programs. Visitors can enjoy the scenic cable car ride that provides unique perspectives of the animals and landscape.

---

## 3. Cincinnati Zoo & Botanical Garden
**Location:** Cincinnati, Ohio

One of the oldest zoos in the nation, the Cincinnati Zoo & Botanical Garden celebrated its 150th anniversary in 2025. Famous as the "Home of Fiona" the hippo, this historic zoo combines animal exhibits with beautiful botanical gardens. It's known for bringing visitors nose-to-nose with incredible wildlife experiences.

---

## 4. Columbus Zoo and Aquarium
**Location:** Columbus, Ohio

The Columbus Zoo and Aquarium is consistently ranked among the top zoos in America. It features expansive animal habitats, a modern aquarium, and innovative exhibits. The zoo is known for its commitment to animal welfare and conservation education.

---

## 5. San Diego Zoo
**Location:** San Diego, California

The San Diego Zoo is one of the most famous zoos in the world and a top-ranked facility in the United States. Known for its innovative open-air enclosures and extensive animal collection, the zoo is a leader in wildlife conservation and education.

---

## 6. Bronx Zoo
**Location:** New York, New York

Located in the Bronx, New York City, the Bronx Zoo is one of the largest metropolitan zoos in the world. It features diverse animal exhibits, including the Congo Gorilla Forest and the Himalayan Highlands, and is renowned for its conservation efforts.

---

## 7. Saint Louis Zoo
**Location:** St. Louis, Missouri

The Saint Louis Zoo is a premier zoo offering free general admission and features over 18,000 animals. Known for its beautiful grounds and innovative exhibits, the zoo is a leader in animal care and conservation.

---

## 8. Denver Zoo
**Location:** Denver, Colorado

Denver Zoo is known for its engaging animal exhibits and commitment to conservation. The zoo features a variety of habitats including Northern Colorado, Africa, and South America sections, providing visitors with diverse wildlife experiences.

---

## 9. Riverbanks Zoo and Garden
**Location:** Columbia, South Carolina

Riverbanks Zoo and Garden earned national recognition on Newsweek's 2025 list of the top 10 zoos in the United States. The facility combines zoo exhibits with beautiful botanical gardens and is known for its animal care and educational programs.

---

## 10. North Carolina Zoo
**Location:** Asheboro, North Carolina

The North Carolina Zoo is one of the largest natural habitat zoos in the world. Featuring African and North American animal habitats, the zoo is dedicated to providing spacious, naturalistic environments for its animals while promoting conservation and education.

---
